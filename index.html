Oh, so you want to know more about me?

I hate Windows. And Microsoft. And Google. And mostly anything tech-related in the $CURRENT_YEAR. No, it's nothing personal. It's just that I follow these principles, which I find are being horribly abused by many tech corporations:

  • Avoid bloat. Hence this website's style which is probably causing some 20-year-old hipster web designer to pull out his hair because I didn't include 17mb of JQuery UI to animate a div or whatever. The simpler the better (Textpunk is a bit extreme, but I agree with some of their intentions.)

  • Open source always. FOSS/FLOSS? Even better!
